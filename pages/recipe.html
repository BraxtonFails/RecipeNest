Blueberry Pancakes

4.9 rating 35 minutes Medium difficulty

Fluffy, buttery pancakes loaded with fresh blueberries and topped with warm syrup. Perfect for a cozy weekend breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter until combined.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry and stir just until no large streaks of flour remain.
  4. Gently fold in blueberries.
  5. Heat a lightly buttered skillet over medium heat.
  6. Pour ¼ cup of batter for each pancake and cook until bubbles form and edges look set, then flip.
  7. Serve warm with syrup and extra blueberries on top.
